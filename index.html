Pest & Disease Guides

Quick guides for common issues. Add more anytime by duplicating a card.

Fall Armyworm (Spodoptera frugiperda)

Windowing on leaves, frass in whorl, ragged defoliation.

Prevention & Treatment

Scouting: Inspect 20–30 plants per field weekly; look for larvae & frass in whorl.

Non-chemical: Hand-pick larvae; apply ash/soil-sand mix in whorl; encourage natural enemies.

Chemical (as last resort): Use recommended active ingredients per local extension advice; rotate modes of action.

Maize Lethal Necrosis (MLN)

Chlorotic mottling, leaf necrosis, poor ear fill; spread by vectors.

Prevention & Management

Seed & Hygiene: Use certified seed; rogue & destroy infected plants.

Vector control: Manage thrips/aphids early; avoid overlapping crops.

Resistant varieties: Where available, plant tolerant hybrids; rotate crops.

Maize Streak Virus (MSV)

Fine yellow streaks on leaves; stunting; spread by leafhoppers.

Prevention & Management

Planting time: Plant early with rains to escape high vector pressure.

Varieties: Use tolerant/resistant varieties if available; remove volunteer maize.

Field hygiene: Control grasses & weeds that host vectors.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Maize Shield work offline?

Yes. After first install, core guides are available offline. Some features (e.g., updates) need internet.

Is it free?

The app is free for smallholder farmers during pilot. Partner features may require agreements.

Which phones are supported?

Android 7.0 (Nougat) and above. iOS support is planned.
